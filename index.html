About Me

Me.

Hi I'm Zhong Guan. I'm a 4th-year undergraduate student in the Department of Electronic & Information Engineering at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University until the end of 2019.

Technology has often found ways to pull me into new hobbies. I got into photography out of a curiosity for DSLR cameras. I grew a reading habit after experiencing the convenience of a Kindle. And I was drawn into making robots out of an initial curiosity with ROS.

I love creating things and always try to make things better, which is probably the reason why I aim to be a professional engineer in my future. My research interests lie in mobile robot dynamics and control, motion planning, computer vision, and machine learning.
